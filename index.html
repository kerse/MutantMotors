MutantMotors

MutantMotors is an interactive simulation of a genetic algorithm. Virtual cars with unique designs (chassis and wheels are determined by "genes") learn to travel as far as possible on a randomly generated track. The best survive, their genes are mixed and mutated, forming increasingly adapted models.

Evolving Parameters:

  • Number and shape of chassis vertices (3-8)
  • Overall chassis scale (0.5-2.0x)
  • Number of wheels (0-4)
  • Radius, ovality, and attachment point of each wheel

You can adjust evolution parameters like population size, mutation intensity, elitism rate, and selection type. Populations can not only progress but also stagnate—sometimes evolution leads to a "dead end" where all designs become similar and further progress halts.
